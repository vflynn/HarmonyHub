Relaxing Playlists


Content

Without realising it, most people use music to calm down or relax. Music is an accessible tool which greatly helps with reducing stress and anxiety. For example, listening to music can lower your heart rate, increase the release of dopamine which makes you feel happy and motivated. Furthermore, listening to music can control your nervous system, which can aid in your ability to fall asleep quickly, can distract you from the things stressing you, boost your mood, promote creativity, improve concentration, and reduce your cortisol levels(your stress hormone).
